This is a project I began during my Senior Year with the goal of developing a machine learning lower-arm prosthetic. The idea is that an apparatus can learn from the user's remaining arm and mirror its usage in the prosthetic. In addition, the arm will be able to employ the same system used in AIVA to recognize the user's voice input. The reason for this is I am trying to design the hand in such a way that it can hold writing devices and, given a command, can hand-write words on a paper or similar medium.
