Learning Extremeties And Prostheses

LEAP is an idea I am working on for a lower-arm prosthetic that learns from the user's remaining arm movements. I am hashing out the designs and code right now but if you'd like to see my test run with myo-electrodes please visit my Youtube Channel. The "learning" aspect of the arm refers to an apparatus that is placed on the user's remaining arm that detects electrical signals in the muscles as well as finger movements in order to learn correspondence that can be applied in the prosthetic.
